Our Story

AmeriStone TX is a family-owned business built on hard work, integrity, and pride in American craftsmanship.

The owner of AmeriStone TX brings over 20 years of experience in the electric utility industry, where he developed a specialized set of skills focused on safety, precision, and doing the job the right way. That experience carries into every flagpole we build and install, from proper site preparation to a safe, professional installation.

We specialize in custom, high-end steel flagpole construction and installation. Every project is handled personally — no shortcuts, no mass production, and no one-size-fits-all solutions. Each flagpole is built and installed with the same care we would expect on our own property.

Behind this business is the constant support of family. AmeriStone TX is supported by the owner’s wife and their two children, who are the reason this work is done with such care and pride. As a family-run operation, our name and reputation mean everything.

Our work is guided by values that never change: God. Family. America.
