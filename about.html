Our Story

AmeriStone TX is a family-owned business built on integrity, and pride in American craftsmanship.

The owner of AmeriStone TX brings over 20 years of experience in the electric utility industry. He has developed a specialized set of skills focused on safety, integrity, precision, and craftsmanship in a enviroment with zero room for error. We believe the support structure should be crafted with all the American flag represents.

We specialize in custom, high-end steel flagpole construction and installation. Every project is handled personally — no shortcuts, no mass production, and no one-size-fits-all solutions. Each flagpole is built and installed with the same care we would expect on our own property.

AmeriStone TX is built on family. The owner’s wife and two children are the heart behind the work we do and the reason every project is handled with care and pride. As a family-run business, our name and reputation mean everything. We are forever grateful for all the men and women who have served our country.

Our work is guided by values that never change: God. Family. America.
